About our workshops

We run a wide range of engaging, hands-on workshops where we live code and customise on the fly for our learners. Workshops are about the experience - not recycling the same dry content.

You can find all our workshops on TicketTailor, they roughly fall into these two buckets.

Data visualisation (tool agnostic)

Our dataviz workshops are practical, focused and driven by quantitative research into what works (and why). We promise you’ll have a unique experience.

R Language Workshops

We’ve got R workshops on {ggplot2}, {shiny}, and more! Come along to our unique, hands-on workshops and we promise you’ll catch our enthusiasm for R.
